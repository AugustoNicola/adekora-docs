Vowels¶

Phonetic Inventory¶

Adèkora has the following 7 monophthongs and 6 diphthongs:

Front Central Back
Close i u
Near-close f
Close-mid f
Mid e̞ o̞
Open-mid ɛː
Near-open f
Open ä ɑː
Diphthongs
äi äu
e̞i e̞u
o̞i o̞u
  • [ i ] as in "window", "asimilar"
  • [ u ] as in "boot", "lúdico"
  • [ e̞ ] as in "let", "medicina"
  • [ o̞ ] as in "Cambodia", "locura"
  • [ ɛ ] as in "dress", -
  • [ ä ] as in "trap", "marca"
  • [ ɑ ] as in "palm", -
